Questions, answered

What do I need to scan a negative?

Just your phone and any light — a window, a lamp, or a lightbox. Hold the negative up to the light and photograph it. No scanner or special hardware required.

Does it work with color and black-and-white?

Both. Color negatives carry an orange film-base mask — the app removes it and inverts the image. Black-and-white negatives invert cleanly too.

Will the colors come out perfect?

Color varies from film to film, so Negative Scanner gives you a great starting point, then easy exposure and white-balance sliders let you nudge skin tones and colors until they look right.

Are my scans uploaded?

Never. Everything happens on your iPhone — no servers, no accounts, no cloud.

Is it a subscription?

No. One payment unlocks unlimited scans forever. No ads, no watermark.
