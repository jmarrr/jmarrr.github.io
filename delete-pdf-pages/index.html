Questions, answered

How do I delete pages?

Open a PDF, tap the pages you want to remove (they turn red), and tap Delete. Then save the new, smaller PDF or share it. A live count always shows how many pages you'll keep.

Does it change the pages I keep?

No. The pages you keep stay exactly as they were — text, images, annotations, and links are all preserved. Nothing is re-rendered or flattened.

Are my documents uploaded?

Never. Everything happens on your iPhone — no servers, no accounts, no cloud.

Is it a subscription?

No. One payment unlocks unlimited edits forever. No ads, no weekly fees.

What is it good for?

Trimming scans, dropping blank or duplicate pages, splitting out a section, or cutting a long report down to just the pages that matter.
