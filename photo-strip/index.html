Questions, answered

How many photos does a strip hold?

Three or four. Pick them in the order they'll appear, and they stack down the strip like a real photobooth print.

What filters and borders are there?

Filters: Original, B&W, Sepia, Fade, and Warm. Borders: White, Black, or Cream. You can also add a date-style caption along the bottom.

Are my photos uploaded?

Never. Everything happens on your iPhone — no servers, no accounts, no cloud.

Is it a subscription?

No. One payment unlocks unlimited strips forever. No ads, no watermark.

Can I share the strip?

Yes. Save it to Photos or share it anywhere — it exports at full resolution.
