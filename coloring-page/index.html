Questions, answered

What does it make?

Clean black line art on a white background — the outlines of your photo, made to be printed and colored in. You can export it as a print-ready PDF or an image.

Can I control how detailed it is?

Yes. Bold gives simple thick outlines little kids can fill, Standard is the everyday setting, and Fine keeps more detail for older kids and adults.

Can I print it?

Yes — export a PDF sized for paper and send it to any printer, or save the image and print from Photos.

Are my photos uploaded?

Never. Everything happens on your iPhone — no servers, no accounts, no cloud, no weekly credits.

Is it a subscription?

No. One payment unlocks unlimited coloring pages forever. No ads, no watermark.
