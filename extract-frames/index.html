Questions, answered

How many photos will I get?

As many as you want. A 10-second 30 fps clip is about 300 frames — export all of them, every Nth, one per second, or just one. The app shows an estimated count before you extract.

What quality are the photos?

Full quality — each frame is exported at the video's own resolution as a high-quality JPG image.

Are my videos uploaded?

Never. Everything happens on your iPhone — no servers, no accounts.

Is it a subscription?

No. One payment unlocks unlimited exports forever. No ads, no watermark.

What is it good for?

Grabbing the sharpest shot from sports and action clips, thumbnails for your channel, art and animation reference, form checks, and saving that one perfect moment from a video.
